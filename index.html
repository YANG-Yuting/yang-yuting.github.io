Yuting Yang, PhD

📧 yuting.yang@childrens.harvard.edu

I'm a postdoctoral research fellow in the Computational Health Informatics Program (CHIP), Boston Children’s Hospital, Harvard Medical School (HMS), under the guidance of Timothy Miller, PhD and William La Cava, PhD. My current research focuses on the development of foundation models in healthcare. Specifically, I am working on multi-modal foundation models for cardiology medical records. I'm also affiliated with the Congenital Heart Artificial Intelligence (CHAI) Lab, BCH, where I work closely with Joshua Mayourian, PhD, MD.
