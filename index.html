Yuting YANG

📧 yangyuting@ict.ac.cn

I received PhD at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) supervised by Prof. Juan Cao and Prof. Jintao Li. I'm interested in research about how to build trustworthy AI systems (mainly for natural language processing tasks) that are adaptive to dynamic changes in environments. My research is launched from robustness as starting points (adversarial robustness and out-of-distribution robustness).
Currently, I am actively engaged in exploring and addressing the safety challenges posed by Large Language Models (LLMs) (e.g., jailbreak). I am also intrigued by the diverse applications of LLMs across various fields (e.g., biology).
